20 September 2025

You asked me,
“Mera saath kahan tak doge tum?”

I smiled and said,
“Jab tak na kaho ki main hara.”

At the time, it sounded casual.
But I meant it more than I showed.

Days passed.
We talked normally, like nothing had changed.

But some feelings don’t disappear —
they just wait for the right moment.

29 November 2025

That moment came when I finally said what I had been holding back.

I love you.
I didn’t even know why I said it then —
I just knew I couldn’t stay silent anymore.

Yahan har saltanat hai teri, har sultan hai tera
Idhar insaan hai tera, udhar bhagwan hai tera
Zameen se aasmaan tak har jagah gungan hai tera
Ye duniya chal rahi hai aaj tak, ahsaan hai tera

I don’t know what the future holds.
But I know this —
these words, this moment, and this feeling were real.
